I am a Ph.D. candidate and Research Assistant at the Wuhan University, working at the Institute of Artificial Intelligence. My research focuses on reinforcement learning, automatic control, and graph neural networks. I am passionate about advancing intelligent transportation and autonomous systems to improve safety and efficiency.
